About Akuyoma Asinugwo & Partners

A registered firm of Estate Surveyors and Valuers equipped technically and professional to handle all aspects of estate surveying and valuation jobs.

A highly focused team, delivering services efficiently. Our fully computerized operations enable us handle large and complicated data analysis and management. We are registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission with Reg. No. BN2590527.

  • appraisal and management that satisfy client needs

  • To be the foremost player in the real Estate Profession in Nigeria and beyond.
